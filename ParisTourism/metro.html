Metro Time table

Due to the sudden increase in the number of tourists over the past few months, a special tourist metro service "Golden Pass" is introduced for tourists only, running from 1200 to 1600.
The route of Golden pass covers some of the most important areas of Paris.
This is not to be used for daily commute purposes. Passengers need to present tourist pass, else they will not allowed to use the service.
